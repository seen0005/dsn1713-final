What We Are All About

At Hoofbeats & Heartbeats, we bring smiles and a little bit of magic to every celebration! Our gentle, well-loved ponies are perfect for birthdays, community events, and special occasions. We make it easy for families to create unforgettable memories with safe, fun, and hands-on pony experiences.
